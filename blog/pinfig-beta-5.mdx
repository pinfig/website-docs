We are very happy to release beta 5 of Pinfig.

We ship a number of tools to support your workflows:

- Adding AI integration
- PDF reader and annotator
- AI integration with PDF annotator
- Properties tool for adding metadata to notes (cards)
- Tag suggestions in search tool
- Tag suggestions in PDF annotator
- Enhancing editor's content blocks
- Enhancing editor's markdown support

We also fixed many issues, especially for Microsoft OS.
