# Style

Pinfig equips you with a collection of tools to customize the appearance of your note cards and boards. This encompasses features like altering shapes, sizes of cards and links, employing layouts, trees, colors, fonts, and a variety of other options.

Explore the full list of styling tools available within Pinfig's Dock.

To gain a deeper grasp of the diverse styles offered by Pinfig, delve into the comprehensive information under the Styles section.
